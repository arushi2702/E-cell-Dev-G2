Past Events


The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) of IIIT Lucknow supports budding entrepreneurs with mentorship, consultancy, and networking opportunities. With a vision to transform ideas into impactful innovations, we bridge the gap between students, entrepreneurs, and investors.

Events that were a part of our flagship event ENspire ~

  • Monopoly: Inspired by the classic board game, this event encouraged participants to strategize, negotiate, and trade to dominate the market.
  • Mystery Room: A real-life escape room scenario where participants solved puzzles and clues under a time constraint to achieve a specific goal.
  • IPL Auction: A simulation of the IPL (Indian Premier League) auction, where participants acted as team owners, bidding for players within a budget and forming competitive teams.
  • Ad Arena: A competition that tested creativity and marketing skills by challenging participants to create and pitch innovative advertisements for fictional or real products.
